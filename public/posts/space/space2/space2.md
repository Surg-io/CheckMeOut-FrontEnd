**# Study Room**

## What is a Study Room?
A study room is a quiet, dedicated space designed for focused work, group collaboration, and academic discussions. It offers a distraction-free environment for studying, reading, or working on projects.

## Features and Amenities
- **Quiet Environment**: Noise-controlled areas for concentration.
- **Group Study Tables**: Spaces for collaborative work.
- **Whiteboards & Markers**: For brainstorming and planning.
- **Power Outlets & USB Ports**: Convenient charging for devices.
- **Wi-Fi Access**: High-speed internet for research and online meetings.

## Reservation and Access
Study rooms can be reserved in advance through the online system. Walk-in usage is available when rooms are not booked.

## Guidelines
- Maintain a quiet atmosphere.
- Clean up after use.
- Respect reservation times to ensure fair access for others.
