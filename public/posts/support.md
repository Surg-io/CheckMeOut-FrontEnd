## 🛠️ Welcome to Our Makerspace
Our makerspace is a collaborative workshop providing access to tools, technology, and resources for creators. Whether you're working on prototyping, DIY projects, or learning new skills, our facility offers:

- 3D Printers (FDM & SLA)
- Laser Cutters/Engravers
- CNC Machines
- Electronics Workstations
- Woodworking Tools
- Sewing/Textile Equipment
- VR/AR Development Kits

**Basic Usage Flow:**
1. Register for an account
2. Read safety protocolss
3. Complete training course (if required)
4. Reserve equipment/space
5. Check in upon arrival
6. Use equipment responsibly
7. Clean up workspace

## 📅 Reservation System Guide
### How to Book:
1. Log in
2. Select equipment/workspace
3. Choose available time slot
4. Confirm booking
5. Receive email confirmation

### Policies:
- Reservations max 4 hours/day per user
- Cancel ≥24hrs before to avoid penalties
- Late arrivals >15min forfeit reservation
- Members must accompany guests

## ⚠️ Equipment Requirements
### General Rules:
- Complete equipment-specific training
- No unsupervised minors (<16 years)
- PPE required in tool areas (safety glasses, closed-toe shoes)

### Device-Specific Guidelines:
| Equipment      | Requirements              | Restrictions                |
|----------------|---------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Laser Cutter   | Level 2 Certification     | Max 18×32" materials        |
| 3D Printer     | Basic Training            | 8hr max print time          |
| CNC Router     | Supervisor Present        | Hardwoods only              |
| Soldering      | Ventilation On            | Lead-free solder only       |

## 🆘 Troubleshooting & Support
### Common Solutions:
- Equipment errors: Restart device
- Software issues: Reboot workstation
- Material jams: Follow posted guides
- Power issues: Check circuit breakers
