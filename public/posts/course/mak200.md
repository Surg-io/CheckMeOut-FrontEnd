# **Laser Cutting Certification Course**  

## **Course Description**  
This course provides an introduction to **laser cutting and engraving**. Students will learn how to prepare vector files, adjust machine settings, and operate the laser cutter safely.  

By the end of the course, students will be certified to use the laser cutter independently in the makerspace.  

---

## **Course Objectives**  
Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:  
- Understand laser cutting principles, including power, speed, and material interactions.  
- Operate a laser cutter safely and efficiently.  
- Prepare digital files using vector-based design software.  
- Identify and select appropriate materials for cutting and engraving.  
- Troubleshoot common laser cutting issues.  

---

## **Prerequisites**  
There are no formal prerequisites, but familiarity with **vector graphics software (Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW, or Inkscape)** is recommended.  

---

## **Required Materials**  
- **Laptop** (with design software installed, if available)

## **Course Structure & Schedule**  

### **Week 1: Introduction to Laser Cutting**  
- Overview of laser cutter components and functionality.  
- Safety procedures and emergency protocols.  

### **Week 2: Software & Design Preparation**  
- Introduction to vector and raster graphics.  
- File preparation: SVG, DXF, AI formats.  

### **Week 3: Machine Operation & Calibration**  
- Hands-on laser cutter training.  
- Setting power, speed, and material adjustments.  

### **Week 4: Troubleshooting & Project Work**  
- Common cutting and engraving issues.  
- Preparing final projects.  

### **Week 5: Final Project & Certification**  
- Students design and fabricate their own project.  
- Certification assessment based on independent machine operation.  

## **Grading Policy**  
This is a **pass/fail** course based on the following criteria:  

| **Assessment**          | **Weight**  |

| Design Assignment       | 20%  |

To pass the course, students must attend all sessions and demonstrate safe and competent machine operation.
